Who We Are

We are a carefully selected team of local, health-care professionals that insures continuity of service meeting the specific needs of patients 24 hours per day, seven days per week.

The Care We Provide

Millennium Nursing LCC, provides private duty, in-home, health related care. Services range from basic companionship, light housekeeping, cooking and errands to more involved medical support delivered by highly skilled registered nurses. The individual and changing needs of clients are managed and addressed flexibly according to the needs of each client.

Staff Selection

  • Millennium Nursing LCC, is a locally owed and operated business. The owner, Diana Weaver, is a registered nurse and college professor with over 28 years experience in the health care field. Over 11 of those years have been in the Coachella Valley serving in local outpatient settings, hospitals, colleges and home care settings. Her familiarity with the community enables her to recruit and hire the very best health care providers. Her local residency makes a hands-on approach to the caring management of each client's needs possible.
  • Every Millennium Nursing staff member is fully vetted through the interview and background check process and is carefully supervised and supported to insure excellent client care. A pool of highly qualified caregivers, certified nursing assistants, licensed vocational nurses and registered nurses are ready to meet the varied needs and preferences of clients.
